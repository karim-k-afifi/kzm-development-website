Privacy Policy

KZM Development — Games, Apps & AI Solutions

1. Introduction

KZM Development (“we,” “our,” or “us”) develops and publishes mobile games (including FlipFall, BeatDash, and GridFall), mobile applications (including Slumbra), and AI-powered solutions. This Privacy Policy explains what information is and is not collected when you use our products, and how that information is handled.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Our products are designed to collect minimal to no personal data. By using our products, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information Collection

We do not collect personal information. Our products do not require account creation, login credentials, or any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

If you choose to sign in with Google Play Games, your public gaming profile (display name) and game activity (scores, achievements) are shared with Google Play Games Services. This sign-in is entirely optional — all games work fully without it, and all game data can also be stored locally on your device without signing in.

However, third-party advertising services integrated into our products may automatically collect certain non-personal device information for the purpose of serving and optimising advertisements. This may include:

  • Advertising identifiers (e.g., Google Advertising ID)
  • Device type, model, and operating system version
  • IP address (used to infer general geographic region)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

This data is collected and processed by the third-party ad provider, not by KZM Development. Please refer to the Third-Party Services section below for more details.

3. Third-Party Services

Our products use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — We use Google AdMob to display interstitial, rewarded, and banner advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalised or non-personalised ads. You can opt out of personalised advertising through your device settings (Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalisation).

    For full details, please review the Google Privacy Policy and the AdMob & AdSense program policies.
  • Google Play Games Services — Used for leaderboards, achievements, and cloud game saves. Sign-in is optional — all games work fully without it. When signed in, Google may collect your player profile (display name), game scores, achievement progress, and cloud save data. We do not access or store players’ Google account information on our servers. All game data can also be stored locally on your device without signing in.

    This data is handled according to the Google Privacy Policy. Players can manage their Play Games data at https://myaccount.google.com/.

We do not use any analytics SDKs, crash-reporting tools, or other third-party data-collection services beyond AdMob and Google Play Games Services at this time. Note: Not all products use all services listed above. For example, Slumbra uses AdMob only (rewarded ads for sound unlocks) and does not use Google Play Games Services.

4. Data Storage

All app and game data — including scores, progress, settings, unlocked content, saved mixes, and preferences — is stored locally on your device using JSON files (games) or SharedPreferences (apps), protected with integrity verification.

If you choose to sign in with Google Play Games, your game progress may also be saved to Google’s cloud via Play Games Services cloud saves. This cloud data is managed by Google and subject to the Google Privacy Policy. We do not operate our own servers or databases that store user data.

Game data is never transmitted to our servers. If you uninstall a game, all locally stored data is permanently deleted. Cloud save data, if any, can be managed through your Google account.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our products are not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. In compliance with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations, if we become aware that a child under 13 has somehow provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

Because our products do not collect personal information directly, the primary consideration relates to the third-party advertising services described above. AdMob is configured in accordance with Google’s policies regarding child-directed content where applicable.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has been affected, please contact us at the email address provided below.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of our products after any modifications to this policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated terms.
